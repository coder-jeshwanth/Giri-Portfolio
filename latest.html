B&W PERFUME

ABOUT

A modern, user-centric perfume website aims to enhance the fragrance shopping experience by making it personalized, informative, and engaging. The site will blend interactive elements with e-commerce functionality, helping users find scents suited to their preferences and encouraging exploration through curated content and intuitive design

PROBLEM

Many perfume websites lack personalization, making it difficult for customers to find fragrances they like, especially since scent is intangible online. This lack of guidance can lead to abandoned shopping experiences, missed purchases, and frustration due to overwhelming product variety.

SOLUTION

The website will utilize a scent profiling feature that recommends perfumes based on user input about preferred notes, mood, and occasions. Interactive descriptions, user reviews, and educational content will also aid users in making informed choices and foster brand loyalty.

KEY FEATURES

  1. Scent Finder Quiz
  2. Fragrance Notes Filter
  3. Personalized Recommendations

DESIGN PROCESS

This structured approach ensures a highly personalized, visually appealing, and user-friendly website that transforms the online perfume shopping experience.

01.

    Research & Analysis

  • Identify target audience needs and study existing fragrance websites for gaps and strengths.
02.

    Persona Creation

  • Create personas that represent target users (e.g., fragrance enthusiasts, new shoppers) to guide decisions.
03.
    Wireframing & Prototyping
  • Develop wireframes for key pages, including the homepage, product pages
04.

    User Testing

  • Refine design based on testing feedback, focusing on intuitive navigation, visually engaging product displays, and clear calls-to-action.
